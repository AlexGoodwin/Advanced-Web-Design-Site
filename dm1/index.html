This I Believe

Read the Essay

I believe in always improving.

Whether it’s training physically, such as for sports or one’s health, emotionally, wherein one learns from experiences and how to better treat others and themselves, or mentally, such as in growth of knowledge and experience, improvement is what ties each day into the next.

The feeling of accomplishment that comes from evidence of improvement is one of my biggest motivators. If today I am better than yesterday, chances are it’s a good day. If tomorrow I’ll be further along on a project, have a better understanding of a class, or even just have another day of experience in something, that’s often enough to keep me going through the day. Better is better, and better is motivating.
